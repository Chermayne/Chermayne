Repowering London

November 2019 - Present
Energy Garden Project Support & Comms

Repowering London is a non-profit organisation that focuses on renewable community energy. I am currently volunteering on their Energy Garden project that aims to utilise disused Overground spaces by turning them into community gardens, in efforts to tackle climate change, encourage biodiversity in London by creating green corridors, and integrate nature into the everyday lives of London commuters.

I am working with their web designer through a site redesign by mainly focusing on user-experience as well as analysing their site and social media data to inform, build, and execute a comms strategy.

I am also supporting on the project through garden maintenence, partner engagement, and admin.
